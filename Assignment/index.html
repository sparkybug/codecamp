Deserts

Deserts remains one of the most beautiful and amazing yet frightful wonders of nature and creation as a whole.

A desert is simply an expanse of Land full of sand and sandy particles where nothing useful grows apart from Date-palms.

They are usually characterized by very harsh weather conditions of which high humidity is preeminent.

There are several deserts in Africa and the World at large. Important to note, is the fact that the hottest in the Africa region is the Sahara Desert
